Researched June 2026. PayPro Global is a hard no for Swellyo's surf-trip marketplace.

**Why:** Built exclusively for SaaS/software/digital goods. Does not support physical goods, travel, experiences, or services. No documented marketplace/split-payout capability for independent third-party vendors. Israel coverage unconfirmed; no mention of tashlumim or Bit.

**How to apply:** Do not pursue PayPro Global for Swellyo payment infrastructure. Continue with Stripe Connect (already researched and approved).

Key facts for the record:
- Business model: Merchant of Record (MoR) — PayPro is the legal seller, vendor sells TO PayPro
- Product scope: SaaS, software, video games, subscriptions ONLY — explicitly "digital goods only"
- Marketplace split: NOT designed for independent vendor payout; "partners system" and V2V transfers are for co-founders/profit-sharing within the same product, not for paying independent third-party hosts
- Israel: Not listed in payment methods page; no mention of ILS, tashlumim, Bit, or Isracard
- Fees: Custom quote only, revenue-share model, no public rates; payout fees are wire $21, ACH $3, PayPal free (US), Payoneer $2
- Payout schedule: 15th of each month, previous month's balance — no escrow/hold mechanism documented
- Onboarding: Sales-gated (no self-serve), 1-year auto-renewing contract, US companies eligible
