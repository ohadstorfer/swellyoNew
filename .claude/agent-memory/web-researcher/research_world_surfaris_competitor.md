World Surfaris is the leading direct competitor archetype for Swellyo's trip marketplace. They are a human-consultant surf travel agency, not a self-serve platform — which is the key structural difference.

**Why:** Eyal researched them as a benchmark for Swellyo's trip booking and host model.

**How to apply:** Use their pricing tiers, destination coverage, booking flow friction points, and refund policy controversy as design inputs when building Swellyo's trip marketplace. They represent what the "phone call + email" incumbent looks like, and where a self-serve platform like Swellyo creates value.

Key facts:
- Founded 1997, Australian-owned, HQ Gold Coast (Kirra office)
- ~18 staff across offices in AU, NZ, Maldives, Bali, California, Europe, South Africa
- ATAS accredited (# A11488), AFTA member
- Sells boat charters + land resorts — all third-party (owns nothing)
- Pricing: Classic $2,500-3,500/person, Signature mid-tier, Luxury $10,000-20,000+/person
- Mentawai boats: ~$3,000 AUD budget, $4,400+ AUD mid
- Booking flow: inquiry form → human consultant quotes within 24h → balance due 60 days before departure
- No instant online booking
- Payments: non-refundable even if operator cancels (source of major complaints on ProductReview: 2.9/5 rating, 2 reviews)
- Travel insurance mandatory
- Revenue model: commission from operators (not client-side fees); consultants are salaried not individually commissioned
- ~40 Mentawai options alone; destinations: Indonesia, Maldives, Fiji, Samoa, PNG, Vanuatu, Taiwan, Pohnpei

## B2B / Trade Investigation (July 2026)

**1. Trade/agent program:** NO. /trade, /agents, /partners, /operators all return 404. No footer link, no agent login, no wholesale/net-rate language anywhere. Single consumer contact form.

**2. Operator representation/GSA:** PARTIAL YES. They describe themselves as "representing and managing surf charters and resorts all around the world." One documented EXCLUSIVE arrangement: "representing John's Place exclusively in Australia" (source: worldsurfaris.com/stoked/aussie-surf-charters-and-resorts/). For most other operators (Siloina, Mentawai Surf Charters, Mangalui, Nomad etc.) the relationship is non-exclusive distribution — those operators also handle direct bookings and list with other agents (Perfect Wave Travel, True Blue Travel etc.). No formal "GSA" or "exclusive agent" language used broadly.

**3. Services sold TO operators:** NO. No marketing, photography, booking-software, yield, or media services sold to operators found. Product & Systems Coordinator (Sophia) "manages the network of partner operators" — inbound relationship management, not a service product sold outward.

**4. Charter/group coordination as a service:** CONSUMER-FACING ONLY. Their "Crew Finder" (worldsurfaris.com/world-surfaris-crew-finder-small-group-surf-travel/) matches individual surfers to fill group charters — but this is demand-side (surfer finds a boat), not supply-side (operator fills their boat). One consultant specializes in corporate/brand/athlete trips. No tool or service for operators trying to fill their own remaining spots.

**5. Affiliate/referral program:** NO. Not found anywhere.

**6. Sister brands or B2B entities:** NO. Single entity (World Surfaris Pty Ltd). "World Surfaris Bali" appears as a LinkedIn page but is same brand, regional office only. No wholesale arm.
