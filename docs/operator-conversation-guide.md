# Operator Conversation Guide — Israeli Trip Operators

How to talk to an Israeli surf-trip operator about joining Swellyo: what to tell them, the money/fee picture, and what to ask them.

> Note: `[X]%` commission is not decided yet. Some fee/hold details depend on the final gateway (Tranzila lead) and structure — confirm before quoting hard numbers.

## Part 1 — What to TELL the operator
- **List and price your trips in Swellyo.**
- **Travelers pay inside the app** — in shekels, with installments (tashlumim), Bit, or card.
- **You don't need your own payment system / Tranzila account** — Swellyo handles the payment infrastructure. You just connect a bank account + ID. Minutes to onboard.
- **The money is held safely until after the trip, then paid out to you** — protects both you and the traveler.
- **Swellyo takes a [X]% commission.**
- **You stay the legal seller** — you issue the tax invoice (חשבונית מס) to the traveler and handle your own VAT; Swellyo just collects and pays you.

## Part 2 — The money & fees (to explain the cost)
- **How you get paid:** bank transfer in ₪ to your account, after the trip.
- **Payout timing:** ~X days after the trip ends (funds held until then).
- **Fees in the chain:**
  - Card processing: ~1.5–2.4% domestic Israeli cards · ~1.75% Amex · ~2.8% foreign cards.
  - Swellyo commission: **[X]%**.
  - Tashlumim: available to travelers; small financing cost only if you want cash upfront.
  - Bit: supported (note ₪ per-transaction limit).
- **Who pays what:** decide — traveler pays the card fee (common) or operator absorbs it; operator pays the commission.
- **Refunds:** handled from the held funds per the cancellation policy; a non-refundable booking fee covers the processing cost so nobody eats it.

## Part 3 — What to ASK the operator
**Business / legal**
- Are you a registered business? (Osek Patur / Osek Murshe / Ltd) Are you VAT-registered?
- Will you issue the tax invoice (חשבונית מס) to the traveler for the trip? (You are the legal seller.)

**Payout / KYC**
- Bank account details for payouts (ILS).
- ID + business registration (for KYC / AML).

**Their current setup**
- How do you take payments today? (Bit / card / bank transfer / cash)
- Do you offer tashlumim today?
- Average trip price + travelers per trip / per year? (gauges fees + Bit limits)

**⭐ Money-holding & cash flow (the important one)**
- Are you OK with funds being held until after the trip, then paid out?
- **Do you need money *before* the trip to pay your suppliers (hotels, guides, flights)?**
  - This is the real tension: many operators need upfront cash, which conflicts with hold-until-after-trip. You may need a deposit-now / balance-later split. Ask EVERY operator this.

**Refunds / cancellations**
- What is your cancellation policy (free window, fees)?
- How do you handle refunds today?
- Are you OK with refunds coming from the held funds per a set policy?
