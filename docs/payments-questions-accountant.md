# Israel Payments — Questions for the Israeli Accountant / Tax Lawyer

Context: Swellyo Inc (US/Delaware) runs a surf-trip marketplace. Plan = an Israeli entity acts as **payment-collection agent** for Swellyo, collecting ₪ from Israeli travelers via Tranzila/PayPlus, splitting to operators, taking a commission that belongs to Swellyo.

> Use **Herzog / Barnea / Gornitzky** (international tax) for the ⭐ ruling questions. A regular accountant can handle the routine ones.
> ⭐ = make-or-break. Lead with these.

## Entity & structure
- ⭐ Should the Israeli collection role be my **personal Osek Murshe or a Ltd (חברה בע״מ)** — given (a) I currently live in Israel, (b) I *may* move to Argentina later, (c) I'm a Swellyo founder? When should I convert Osek → Ltd?
- Should the entity be owned by me, or by a non-Swellyo Israeli resident (e.g. my grandfather) — and does that materially reduce the PE / liability risk?

## VAT (the big one)
- ⭐ Can you confirm via **pre-ruling** that my entity is a **payment-collection agent** (VAT on my commission/fee only) and **NOT merchant-of-record** (which would be VAT on the FULL trip price)?
- What exact **contract language + invoicing flow** do you need to hold the agent classification? My understanding: the **operator issues the tax invoice (חשבונית מס)** to the traveler for the full trip price; I issue only a **receipt (קבלה)** for collected funds + a separate tax invoice for my own commission. Confirm.
- Must all trip operators be registered **עוסק מורשה**? What happens if an operator is not registered?
- ⭐ Is my **service fee to Swellyo (US) zero-rated as export of services (§30(a)(5))**, or **18%** because Israeli travelers/operators also benefit (dual-beneficiary exclusion)?

## Cross-border / PE / withholding
- ⭐ Does a dedicated Israeli collection agent **that I control** create a **dependent-agent permanent establishment (PE)** for Swellyo Inc — making the commission fully taxable in Israel (23%)? How do we structure to avoid it?
- **Withholding tax (ניכוי מס במקור)** on remitting Swellyo's commission to the US — does it apply? Can the **US–Israel tax treaty get it to 0%**, and what documentation is needed (US Form 6166, ITA Form 2513/2 / אישור ניכוי)?
- Do we need a **s.85A transfer-pricing study** (related parties — I control both sides) and **Form 1385**?

## Regulatory & cost
- Confirm **split-at-source keeps us under the ₪5M/month** aggregator-license exemption (Securities Authority / Regulation of Payment Services Law). Any ISA exempt-entity notification to file?
- Is Swellyo Inc **separately exposed** to Israeli VAT / PE under the **Foreign-Supplier VAT regime (18%, Jan 2025) / Circular 4/2016**, or does this structure handle it?
- **Total cost** to set up + run: registration, the **3 pre-rulings** (cost + timeline), bookkeeping, annual report, and **Bituach Leumi + health tax** on my fee income.

## The 3 pre-rulings to lock (no published ruling exists on this exact structure)
1. **PE / withholding** — does the agent create a taxable presence for Swellyo, killing treaty 0%?
2. **VAT classification** — agent (VAT on commission) vs merchant-of-record (VAT on full price)?
3. **Export-of-services VAT** — 0% or 18% on my service fee?

Budget ~₪10–30k for the opinions/rulings up front — cheap insurance vs a six-figure reclassification.
