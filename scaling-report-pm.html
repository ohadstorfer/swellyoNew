Scaling Review

Will Swellyo hold up as it grows?

A plain-English look at how every part of the app behaves with 1, 10, 100, 1,000 and 10,000 users — what stays smooth, what starts to strain, and what to fix first. No jargon.

Reviewed by an 11-expert team that read the real code · 17 June 2026

The short version

  • ✓
    Today you're fine. At ~500 active users (where you are now) and up to a few hundred, everything feels instant. Nothing needs fixing to keep today's users happy.
  • !
    First strain shows around 1,000 active users. Mostly at busy moments — the trips feed refreshes a lot, chat badges lag for heavy users, and some phone alerts arrive a few minutes late. Annoying, not broken.
  • ✕
    Real trouble lands around 10,000 users. Eight of the eleven parts hit a wall during busy peaks: laggy chats, a flickering trips feed, late notifications, stalled AI replies, and the live "online/instant" connection hitting its ceiling.
  • !
    The good news: almost every red is the same handful of root causes repeated. Fix ~5 things (mostly medium effort) and the app comfortably clears 10,000+.
