## Summary

**`src/screens/ChatScreen.tsx`** (4 fixes):

4. **Input wrapper paddingBottom** — Changed from `35` to `Platform.OS === 'web' ? 35 : 8`. SafeAreaView handles bottom inset on native; 35px was creating excessive bottom space.
